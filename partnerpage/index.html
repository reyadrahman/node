About

Deepiks is a next-generation marketplace for the creative industries. We are not racing to add more images. We are racing to provide premium creative contents, with a premium technology and service.

Deepiks has been created by seasoned entrepreneurs Philippe Bigard and Emmanuel Prat. We believe that curation is the future, and that Artificial Intelligence can be a game changer. We supported and hosted by prestigious Ecole Polytechnique in the Paris-Saclay cluster.
